Smart Farming for Ceylon Pepper

PepperPal is an intelligent digital solution designed to enhance Ceylon Pepper cultivation in Sri Lanka by combining artificial intelligence and local agricultural expertise. This system helps farmers overcome key challenges like disease outbreaks, market price volatility, and nutrient deficiencies.

PepperPal integrates four main modules that work together to provide real-time, data-driven insights and personalized recommendations for each farmer, ensuring sustainable and profitable farming practices.

Core Features

Cutting-edge AI solutions tailored for Ceylon pepper cultivation

Disease Detection

Identify pepper leaf diseases like Yellow Mottle Virus, Vine Borer, Lace Bug, and Thrips using advanced image processing and CNN models. Get instant diagnosis through simple photo uploads.

Price Prediction

Forecast national and district-level pepper prices using LSTM models trained on real market data from 2016 to 2024. Make informed selling decisions with confidence.

Nutrient Deficiency Diagnosis

Detect Calcium, Magnesium, and Potassium deficiencies using MobileNetV2. Receive fertilizer recommendations validated by agricultural experts for optimal plant health.

Variety Recommendation

Get optimal pepper variety suggestions based on soil conditions, rainfall patterns, temperature, and elevation using Random Forest classification for your specific location.

Our Vision & Mission

Driving precision agriculture through AI and farmer-centered innovation

Vision

To revolutionize Sri Lanka's spice farming sector by enabling sustainable, data-driven agriculture through intelligent digital technologies that empower every farmer.

Mission

To empower pepper farmers with accessible, AI-based tools that enhance productivity, reduce risk, and promote long-term agricultural resilience for sustainable farming practices.

Our Impact

By integrating data science and agriculture, PepperPal aims to improve productivity, reduce losses, and support sustainable farming. Farmers gain tools to make informed decisions on disease management, nutrient application, and economic planning—enhancing both yield and income for a more prosperous future.
